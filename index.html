About me

I am originally from Murray, UT, and I am a Junior in Civil Engineering at BYU. My emphasis is in Water Resources and Transportation, and I use python and web programming technologies to do data science and develop data visualization tools for the Hydroinformatics lab in my department. I am interested in improving my programming skills for software and web development, so I can fill a unique niche in my field and have the flexibility to work as a strict developer if that allows me to better live where I want (my wife and I want to live in Germany for a few years at some point).

In addition to programming and engineering, I am passionate about music. I play the cello and the piano, and I also sing in the BYU Men's Chorus. I also love the outdoors and my wife and I try to go rock climbing and backpacking as much as possible during the summers, though we're not very good climbers so it can be tricky to find routes that we can do.

Recipe Finder

I love cooking and often find myself thinking of something I want to eat and just making the first recipe blog post that pops up. Sometimes, however, I know what ingredients I have and feel like using, but need ideas for what to make with those. On top of this, if I find a recipe I really like, it can be tedious to find a place to save a link to it, when I'm in a hurry to just cook and eat. In light of this, I thought it would be cool to set up some kind of querying service to be able to search for recipes in different ways, and an easy way to store and share them seamlessly.
